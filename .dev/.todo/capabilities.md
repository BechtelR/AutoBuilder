## CB-1: Member Role (Non-CEO Human)

**Source**: Phase 8a shaping (2026-04-12)

A "Member" role with permissions distinct from CEO. The supervision hierarchy currently defines agent tiers (CEO → Director → PM → Worker), not human tiers. CEO is the only human role. Member requires: authorization model, queue scope, project visibility rules, approval authority boundaries.

**Blocked by**: Multi-user support graduating from PRD §8 (Out of Scope) to §3 (Functional Requirements). Single-user local deployment is the current constraint (PRD §7).

**Propagation path**: PRD §3 (new requirement) → Architecture (auth model) → BOM (components) → Roadmap (phase assignment, likely 11+)
